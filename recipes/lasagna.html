Lasagna Recipe

This is the lasagna

Cheesy, well baked lasagna serving to be the perfect meal for bulking

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna slices
  • Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Lay lasagna slice
  2. Add chicken, tomato sauce and cheese
  3. Put in the oven for 45 minutes
  4. Take out of the oven and enjoy your meal!
